About

Language is not required for simple observation of reality and to act upon it. All species on earth except humans were able to survive without verbal communication generation after generation. Human beings are the only species on the earth that have escaped this primitive existence and built a society based on intelligent thought.

Construction of an intelligent thought requires formulation of concepts. These concepts are used internally by the mind to observe the reality and then meditate on that reality. Concepts that are formulated by the mind are shared with fellow intelligent minds that are part of cooperative society.

To share concepts which are abstract ideas, humans needed a representation. Hence language, vocabulary and words have become an important tools to accomplish this goal. The purpose of this website is encourage the usage of the words as concepts and understand their deeper meaning.
